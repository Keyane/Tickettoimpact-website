01 Start here · Clarity
For when you can't see the next step

Think Sidestream

Investment from €150 per session. Strategy packages, decision bundles, and ongoing sparring are priced on scope. We'll always be clear about what it costs before we begin.

Your strategic side channel, a place alongside your daily operations to step out, reflect, and make decisions that actually hold. Not theoretical. Not a workshop. A real conversation with someone who understands the difference between motion and movement.

The Business Hub builds things. Think Sidestream decides what's worth building. That distinction matters more than most people realise until they're twelve months in and wondering why the doing hasn't made them feel better.

Sessions are slower by design. We sit with what's actually going on, the business, the life, the things you haven't said out loud yet, and find the real next step together. You leave with focus, not more to-dos.

What's included

Strategic Deep-Dive

  • Zoom out from daily operations
  • Examine direction, positioning and priorities
  • Clarify what to stop doing, often the biggest shift
  • Leave with a clear focus for the period ahead

Offer & Customer Journey

  • Review offers, pricing and structure
  • Map how clients move from first contact to working with you
  • Simplify where things have become unclear over time
  • When the offer is clean, sales and marketing follow naturally

Decisions & Scenarios

  • Explore directions, investments or changes you're weighing
  • Map consequences across team, cashflow and customer impact
  • Stop overthinking and start deciding with overview
  • Calmer decisions, fewer regrets

Ongoing Strategic Sparring

  • Monthly or quarterly sessions that keep you sharp
  • Check in on direction, decisions and drift
  • A thinking partner who knows your business
  • Prevents the slow slide back into reactive mode

Is this a fit?

  • You're moving, but lack direction
  • You want clarity in decisions, not more options
  • You have a solid foundation and want strategic depth
  • You're ready to buy clarity instead of delay

How it's structured

  • Deep-Dives → single sessions or bundles
  • Offer & Journey Design → project-based
  • Decision Labs → bundles of 3–5 sessions
  • Ongoing Sparring → monthly retainer

Think in impact, not in noise

  • Real decisions, not what-if conversations
  • Clarity you can act on the next morning
  • Less overthinking, more direction
  • A business that fits your actual life

High Ticket Sales

Performance-based or project-based. Closing is commission. CRM optimisation and sales architecture are priced per project or hourly. Starts from €1,500 for structural work. We apply a holistic view to every investment, including yours.

For when the offer is real, the foundations hold, and it's time for fewer, better conversations with the right people. Sales that feel like care, not pressure.

This goes beyond appointing a closer or generating meetings. We design and guide a fully integrated sales process, where lead generation, qualification, closing, and CRM structure work together as one system. No loose ends. No lost leads.

The approach is built on transparency and trust. No pressure tactics, no tricks, just clear decisions and professional guidance that protects your reputation while growing your revenue.

Appointment Setting

  • Carefully selected prospects aligned with your offer and values
  • Not volume, fit

High Ticket Closing

  • Relationship-first approach built on trust
  • No pressure tactics, no tricks
  • Clear decisions and professional guidance
  • Conversations that feel right to both sides

CRM Structure & Follow-up

  • Build or optimise your CRM from the ground up

Full Sales Architecture

  • Predictable process that scales with you
