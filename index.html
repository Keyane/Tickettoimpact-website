Our story

We didn't start as a company. We started as a family.

We were both running our own businesses, each with our own clients, projects and responsibilities. Over time, our paths naturally merged into one direction.

Before that, we spent years working in healthcare and government. Years of responsibility, problem-solving, working with people, systems and organisations. Valuable experience that taught us how to listen, understand what lies beneath the surface, and create structure where it matters most.

Then we made a different choice.

We chose freedom over routine and moved from the Netherlands to the Algarve. Not because we wanted to work less, but because we wanted work to fit life, not the other way around.

That shift taught us what truly sustainable growth looks like. The slow mornings. The difficult decisions. Building a business while raising a family. Creating something meaningful without sacrificing everything else that matters.

Today, that's exactly what we help others do.

Not just with websites, systems, sales, communication or operations, but by helping create a business that supports the life behind it. Because the person behind the business matters just as much as the business itself.

Nature, animals, family and genuine connection are what give us energy. We bring that same approach into our work: practical, honest, calm and human.

Because businesses grow best when they're built around people, not around pressure.
