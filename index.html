Our story

We didn't start as a company. We started as a family.

We chose freedom over routine, from the Netherlands to the Algarve, and built our work around life, not the other way around.

That shift taught us everything about what it means to build something that truly fits. The slow mornings. The hard conversations. The bet that you can do meaningful work and still be home for dinner.

Now we help others do the same.
